# OCNJ.ETH Business Operations and Package Plan

## Recommended launch model

Use a curated media-and-directory model rather than selling ordinary banner ads.

### 1. Community Listing — Free

Includes:

- Business name
- Category
- Official website
- Map link
- Basic directory placement

Purpose:

- Makes the guide useful.
- Builds goodwill with the business community.
- Creates a lead pool for upgrades.

### 2. Founding Featured Partner — $149 per month or $399 prepaid for three months

Use this as the launch offer for the first 10 businesses.

Includes:

- Expanded business profile
- Priority category placement
- Up to five approved photos
- One current offer, event or seasonal message
- Eligibility for the rotating homepage feature
- Clearly labeled “Featured Partner” placement
- One update per month

Recommended rule:

- Three-month minimum, or charge $149 month-to-month.
- Founding price remains available while the business remains continuously subscribed.
- Raise the public price after the first 10 partners.

### 3. Signature Homepage Feature — $500 for two weeks

Includes:

- One primary homepage carousel slide
- Direct link to the business
- Approved image and promotional copy
- “Featured Partner” disclosure
- Optional inclusion in one newsletter

Limit availability:

- One paid feature at a time.
- No more than two sponsored slides in the carousel.
- Do not sell competing businesses the same exclusive period.

### 4. Seasonal Presenting Partner — $1,500 to $2,500 per season

Best for:

- Hotels
- Vacation-rental companies
- Realtors
- Large attractions
- Multi-location restaurants
- Banks or professional services

Includes:

- Homepage recognition
- Seasonal guide sponsorship
- Dedicated editorial-style partner story
- Newsletter placement
- Category exclusivity when priced appropriately
- End-of-season performance summary

## Recommended pricing path

### First 90 days

- Free listing
- $149 monthly Featured Partner
- $500 two-week Homepage Feature
- $1,500 Seasonal Presenting Partner

### After traffic and subscriber growth

When the site has credible traffic, email subscribers and business results:

- Raise Featured Partner to $199–$249 per month.
- Raise Homepage Feature to $750–$1,000 for two weeks.
- Price category exclusivity separately.
- Add paid newsletter sponsorship.
- Add professionally produced photo/story packages.

## Best payment setup

Use Stripe Payment Links at launch.

Create separate links for:

1. Featured Partner monthly subscription
2. Featured Partner three-month prepaid package
3. Homepage Feature one-time payment
4. Seasonal Presenting Partner deposit or invoice

Stripe Payment Links can sell one-time products and recurring subscriptions without custom code.

## What happens after a business pays

### Simple launch workflow

1. Business selects a package.
2. Business pays through Stripe.
3. Stripe sends the receipt and records the customer.
4. Redirect the customer to an onboarding form.
5. Collect:
   - Legal business name
   - Public business name
   - Contact name and email
   - Website
   - Address
   - Logo and photographs
   - Requested promotion
   - Package start date
   - Confirmation that submitted materials may be published
   - Agreement to the advertising terms
6. Review the business before publishing.
7. Prepare the feature.
8. Send a preview.
9. Publish after approval.
10. Record the start and expiration dates.
11. Remove or renew placement when the term ends.

## Recommended onboarding tools

At launch, use one of:

- Tally
- Google Forms
- Jotform
- Formspree

Set Stripe’s successful-payment redirect to the onboarding form.

## What should not be automatic at launch

Do not automatically publish a business immediately after payment.

Reasons:

- The business could submit unsuitable material.
- The site needs consistent quality.
- Competitor conflicts may exist.
- The requested claim could be inaccurate.
- Paid placement needs proper disclosure.
- A refund or correction may be required.

State that placements generally go live within two or three business days after complete materials are received.

## Subscription management

For recurring packages:

- Use Stripe subscriptions.
- Enable Stripe’s customer portal.
- Let customers update payment methods or cancel through Stripe.
- Keep the placement live only while payment is current.
- Add a short grace period for failed payments.
- Keep screenshots and records of what was delivered.

## Editorial separation

A business can pay for:

- Placement
- Photography
- Storytelling
- Distribution
- Sponsorship
- Newsletter exposure

A business should not be able to purchase:

- A guaranteed positive review
- A guaranteed score
- A false “local favorite” designation
- Removal of truthful criticism
- Undisclosed advertising

Paid homepage items must use:

```javascript
sponsored: true
```

## Terms to publish before taking money

Create clear advertising terms covering:

- What the package includes
- Start and end dates
- Renewal and cancellation
- Refund policy
- Creative approval
- Deadlines for submitting materials
- Prohibited claims or content
- Right to reject or remove content
- No guaranteed traffic, sales or ranking
- Sponsored-content disclosure
- Intellectual-property permission
- Business responsibility for accuracy
- Limitation of liability

Have a Pennsylvania attorney review the terms before meaningful revenue or long-term commitments.
